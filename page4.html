SDG 13: Climate Action

Using Artificial Intelligence to fight climate change

Sustainable Development Goal 13 focuses on taking urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. Climate change affects weather patterns, sea levels, ecosystems, and human life across the world.

Artificial Intelligence helps scientists and governments analyse climate data, predict extreme weather events, and monitor environmental changes more accurately.

AI is used in climate modelling, disaster prediction, energy efficiency, and reducing carbon emissions, supporting faster and smarter climate action worldwide.

  • Predicts floods, cyclones, and heatwaves
  • Monitors deforestation and melting glaciers
  • Improves renewable energy efficiency
  • Helps reduce greenhouse gas emissions
